Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 7, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how our WhatsApp Bot (“the Bot”) processes your information when you interact with it. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how your data is handled.

2. Information We Process

  • When you send a message to the Bot, the content of your message is received temporarily to generate a response.
  • Your WhatsApp number is used solely to deliver replies.
  • No databases or long-term storage are used. Messages are only processed in real-time.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To understand your message and generate a reply.
  • Optionally, your message may be processed by Google’s Gemini AI API to generate a response.
  • Replies are then sent back to you through the WhatsApp Cloud API (Meta).

4. Data Retention

We do not permanently store your messages or personal data. Any information is processed in real-time and discarded after a response is generated. Debug logs may temporarily print incoming messages to the server console but are not saved in any database.

5. Sharing of Information

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.
  • Your data may be transmitted to Meta (WhatsApp Cloud API) and Google (Gemini AI API) for processing, in accordance with their own privacy policies.

6. Third-Party Services

Our Bot relies on third-party services whose privacy practices apply:

  • Meta (WhatsApp) Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

7. Your Rights

Since no personal data is stored, you do not need to request deletion. If you stop interacting with the Bot, no further processing occurs.
